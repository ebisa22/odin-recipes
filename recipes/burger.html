Burger Recipe

A burger recipe involves grilling or pan-frying a seasoned beef patty until juicy and browned. It's typically served in a toasted bun with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and condiments. Easy to customize with toppings or sauces to your taste.

Recipes to follow to make a great burger

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt & pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Ketchup, mayo, mustard (optional)

Steps

  1. Season ground beef and form patties.
  2. Cook patties in a pan or on grill.
  3. Toast buns lightly.
  4. Assemble with cheese and toppings.
  5. Serve with condiments of choice.
