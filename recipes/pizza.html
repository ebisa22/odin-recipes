Pizza Recipe

Recipes to follow to make a great pizza

A pizza recipe starts with a simple dough base topped with sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings. It's baked at high heat until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Great for customizing with meats, veggies, or herbs.

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano or basil (optional)
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out dough on floured surface.
  3. Spread sauce and add cheese & toppings.
  4. Bake for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve hot.
