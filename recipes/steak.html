Steak Recipe

A steak recipe is a method for preparing a juicy, flavorful cut of beef, typically seared on high heat to form a caramelized crust. It is often seasoned simply with salt and pepper to highlight the meat's natural flavor. Depending on preference, it can be cooked rare to well-done and paired with sauces, vegetables, or potatoes.

Recipes to follow to make a great steak

Ingredients

  • Steak meat
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pat steak dry and season with salt & pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over high heat.
  3. Sear steak 2-3 min per side.
  4. Add butter, garlic, herbs; baste steak.
  5. Rest for 5 min before serving.
